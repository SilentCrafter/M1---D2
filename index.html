Foccacia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vargas

Featured in:

Fresh Cranberry Recipies That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the Focaccia:

  • 4 cups of All-purpose Flour
  • 1.5 Teaspoon of Instant yeast
  • 1.5 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 1.75 cups of Water
  • 0.25 cups of Extra virgin olive Oil

For the Topping:

  • 3 Medium Onions
  • 1 Tablespoon of Vinegar
  • 2 Tablespoons of Olive Oil for brushing
  • 10 Ounces of Brie
  • 1.5 Cups of Cranberries

Method

1. Making The Focaccia Dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.
